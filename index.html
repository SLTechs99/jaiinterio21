    Our Work Process

    1

    Consultation

    Always we start with the Meet to Get to know you, Learn what you like and what inspires you.
    2

    Feasibility Check

    We will do preliminary studies of the site for the design along with your timescales & budgets.
    3

    Concept Discussion

    We will show you ideas, colors, furnishings, design options you couldn’t imagine on your own.
    4

    Design Plan

    At this point we will present our designs to you for your approval before moving to our next step.
    5

    Design Finalization

    We will prepare the final drawings to avoid unnecessary delays related to indecision or repeated changes.
    6

    Procurement

    Now the tender process begins, we work with the team to advise on the best form of contract & contractors.
    7

    Execution

    We will ensure builders and contractors to follow the plan and will address any challenges.
    8

    Evaluation

    A walk-through will address any last-minute issues, then we’ll leave you to enjoy your dream home!.
